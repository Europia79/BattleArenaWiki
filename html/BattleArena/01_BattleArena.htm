Running Competitions that have a JoinPhase

Unlike a queue that can be joined at any time, and will be started whenever enough people join. Competitions with a Join Phase need to be opened.

When they are opened they are announced to the server, and then will start after a given amount of time for everyone that has joined. By default the competitions that do this are the FreeForAll, Deathmatch, and Tournament.

Starting games that have a join phase : FreeForAll and Deathmatches (by default)

Scheduling Events : How to schedule games that have a join phase to be run continuously.
